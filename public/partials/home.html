Welcome to Basketball Radar

See a list of all players within the database. Select individual players to see a breakdown of their combine metrics. Players listed from 1987 and forward. By selecting an individual player, it will link to a unique radar chart for that player's specific combine data.

Use d3 generated radar chart to either view one specific player, or compare two players and visualy see how they stand up next to one another.

My reason for creating this application was in an effort to see if there was a way of potentially predicting an NBA player's career arc based off of their combine measurements. On the about page you will find a table detailing a few of the combine tests, and their associated statistic. Also a list of ways to contact me.
